How it works

SuburbRank turns suburb metrics into a clear ranking model.

1. Data inputs

Population growth, price momentum, yield, vacancy, supply trends, and infrastructure inputs feed the model.

2. Scoring engine

Each suburb is normalized, weighted, scored, and ranked against the NSW universe.

3. Investor output

The highest-ranked suburbs appear in the dashboard, suburb profiles, and weekly reports.
